Tuna Patties

Easy to make breaded Tuna Patties

Ingredients

  • 3 cans of Tuna (in Water)
  • Galic season bread crumbs
  • egg
  • dice tomato, onion
  • flour
  • salt , pepper

Steps

  1. Open Tuna Can and place content in a bowl
  2. crack egss open a pour into a seperate bowl and scramble up
  3. dice up the tomato
  4. dice up the onions
  5. Pour some flour in a plate
  6. Pour some Bread Crumbs spread on a seperate plate
  7. Mix the diced tomatoes and Onions into the same bowl the Tuna is in
  8. Mix content together and roll in a ball
  9. Take tuna ball out of the and batter it in the eggs until its covere fully
  10. Than batter the Tuna Ball with flour (just a little bit , so its slight
